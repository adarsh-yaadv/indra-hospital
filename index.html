About Indra Hospital

A decade of compassionate, affordable, and quality healthcare for the people of Sant Kabir Nagar and beyond.

Our Story

Built on a Promise to Serve

Indra Hospital was established with a singular purpose: to make quality, dignified healthcare accessible to every family in Eastern Uttar Pradesh — regardless of their economic background.

Located in Kali Jagdishpur, Sant Kabir Nagar, we have grown from a small clinic into a fully equipped multi-specialty hospital, serving thousands of patients from across the region every year.

Our team of qualified doctors, dedicated nurses, and support staff work around the clock — united by one goal: your health and wellbeing.

Foundation
Hospital Established
Indra Hospital opened its doors in Kali Jagdishpur with a small team of dedicated doctors and a 20-bed facility.
Expansion
Emergency & ICU Wing Added
Recognizing the critical need for emergency services, a 24×7 casualty and ICU was established.
Growth
Maternity & Pediatrics Wing
A dedicated maternity ward and children's care unit was inaugurated, serving hundreds of mothers annually.
Present
Multi-Specialty Hospital
Today, Indra Hospital is a trusted multi-specialty center with 9 departments, 15+ doctors, and 50,000+ patients served.
